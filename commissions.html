FAQ

Common questions

How long does a commission take?+
Typically 2–6 weeks depending on the medium and complexity. Oil takes longer due to drying time. Watercolour and graphite are faster.
Do you ship outside India?+
Currently commissions are for India only. Get in touch to discuss options for international delivery.
Can I request a specific size?+
Yes! Size is discussed during the enquiry process. Larger sizes may affect the price — especially for oil and acrylic.
What information do I need to provide?+
Share your idea, reference images if any, preferred medium, size, and timeline. The more detail the better!
